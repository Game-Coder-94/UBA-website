Student Participation

Learning beyond the classroom

Why Join UBA?

Unnat Bharat Abhiyan offers students a unique opportunity to apply their technical knowledge to solve real-world problems. By participating, students gain hands-on experience in community development, project management, and social entrepreneurship.

Skills You Will Gain:

  • Leadership & Teamwork
  • Social Surveying & Data Analysis
  • Technical Problem Solving
  • Communication & Empathy

How to Participate

1

Register

Sign up with the UBA Cell at the beginning of the academic session.

2

Volunteer

Join field visits for household and village surveys.

3

Innovate

Propose and work on projects to address identified issues.
