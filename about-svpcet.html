Our Role

The UBA Cell at SVPCET is dedicated to bridging the gap between technical education and rural development. Established to foster social responsibility among engineering students, the cell actively engages with adopted villages to identify grassroots problems and propose sustainable engineering solutions.

We believe that technology should serve the common man. By involving students in field visits, surveys, and project implementation, we aim to create a generation of socially aware engineers.

Collaboration

The success of our UBA initiatives relies on strong collaboration between:

  • Students: Who bring energy, innovation, and technical skills.
  • Faculty: Who provide guidance, mentorship, and technical expertise.
  • Village Community: Who are the primary stakeholders and partners in development.
  • Local Administration: Who support implementation of government schemes.
