Engineering Solutions for Rural Development

Low-Cost Converted E-Bicycle

Recognized by IIT Delhi Panjari Lodhi Village

Students developed a conversion system to transform conventional bicycles into hybrid electric bicycles for Panjari Lodhi Village. This project addresses the need for an affordable and eco-friendly mobility solution, reducing transportation costs by expected 40-50%.

Key Features:

  • Hybrid Electric Conversion of old bicycles
  • Affordable & Eco-Friendly
  • Promotes economic empowerment & accessibility
Guide: Dr. Ashutosh Verulkar

Smart Solar Street Lamp

Recognized by VNIT Nagpur Khatmari Village

Successfully installed in Khatmari village, this smart lamp features an automatic solar panel cleaning system, battery management, and anti-theft alarm. The cleaning system improves solar efficiency by 15-25%.

Key Features:

  • Automatic Solar Panel Cleaning
  • Automatic Night Light Activation
  • Anti-Theft Alarm
Guide: Dr. Ashutosh Verulkar | Team: Atharva Hampiholi, Ashutosh Maske & Team

Automated Urea Spraying Drone

Agriculture Khatmari Village

Designed for Khatmari Village, this project leverages drone technology and machine learning to optimize urea spraying, reducing manual labor and resource wastage. It aims to improve crop yields and economic well-being.

Key Features:

  • Drone-Based Fertilization
  • High-Resolution Data Collection (Soil moisture, crop health)
  • Machine Learning Optimization
Guide: Prof. Priti Golar | Team: Dipmala Kathale, Saurav Nerkar & Team

SVPCET Gram Arogya Seva App

Healthcare Software

A Virtual Doctor App developed to improve rural healthcare accessibility. It features Patient, Doctor, and Pharmacy modules for online appointments, consultations, and medicine ordering.

Key Features:

  • Secure login & doctor profile search
  • Appointment tracking & Virtual consultations
  • Prescription management
Guide: Dr. Pallavi Wankhede | Team: Rajlin Kaur, Vedant Nisar & Team

Smart Agro-Bot

Precision Farming IoT

IoT-Based Seed Sowing Agro-Bot for Kharif Crops. Integrates IoT, GPS, and sensors to automate seed sowing, optimize placement, and monitor soil moisture/pH.

Key Features:

  • IoT, GPS & Sensor Integration
  • Web-Based Remote Control
  • Environmental Adaptation
Guide: Dr. Komal Gehani | Team: Abhishek Wasekar, Nupendra Waghmare & Team
