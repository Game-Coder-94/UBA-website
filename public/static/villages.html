Adopted Villages

Communities we serve and grow with

SVPCET UBA Cell has adopted five villages in the Nagpur district. Our objective is to identify the problems in these villages and provide sustainable technological solutions.

Gavsi Manapur

Nagpur District, Maharashtra

Gavsi Manapur is one of the localities in Nagpur (Pin: 441108). It is located near Panjari Farm, Jamtha and Rui. Being the closest adopted village to the college, it serves as a primary hub for our pilot projects and frequent student visits.
